If you’re too busy to get to the grocery store, there’s many ways for the grocery store to come to you! Online grocery shopping has been rapidly expanding over the past several years, with lots of different websites providing groceries shipped right to you. Online grocery shopping can save you time, stress, gas money, and expose you to new types of foods and brands you can’t get at your local grocery store.

Pineapple Market is a brand new online stores and has very good prices as well as a good selection of specialty items, organic, and also carries many health and beauty items. This store does offer perishable items such as hot dogs and dairy. Pineapple Market will deliver to APO and FPO addresses at variable rates depending on the weight of what you’ve purchased.
